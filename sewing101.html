Welcome to Hand Sewing 101

In this guide, you will learn the basics of threading a needle.

First, gather your supplies.

  • One large needle
  • Cotton thread with a color of your choice
  • Thread snips or scissors

Next, follow the step by step guide below.

  • 1. Prepare Your Thread

    First, a section of thread the length of your arm.

  • 2. Threading the Needle

    Next, hold your thread in your non-dominant hand and the needle in the dominate hand. You will then place the eye of your needle over the tip your thread.

  • 3. Tying Off

    Lastly, maneuver your needle so that it is at the center of your length of thread. Grab the ends of your thread together and tie a squre knot.

You are now ready to start hand sewing!
